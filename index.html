Space Ships

State: Open Alpha

Space Ships is a multiplayer, first-person shooter in space. Space Ships allows a team of players to drive their pirate ship around in an open space arena with the goal to destory the opposing team's ship.

This game introduces a rare mechanic where the players are not only free to traverse around their own ship, but can disembark and embark at any time.

As the only developer, I had to design and implement:

  • Gameplay
  • Movement
  • Physics
  • Replication
  • Multiplayer
  • UI
